    Buying Items in a Store

    A demo showcasing an RPG-style store with a number of items available to purchase

    In this demo, we showcase the custom Unlock integration, allowing the user to connect with MetaMask or WalletConnect and display their ETH balance and account details all using native Unity UI. Once connected, there's a number of chests, with keys available to purchase as NFTs using Unlock Protocol. Once a purchase is completed, the state of the chest is updated visually to let the player know they now have access.
